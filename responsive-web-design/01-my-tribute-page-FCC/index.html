Don Norman

American researcher, professor, and author.

Short time line:

  • 1935 - Donald Arthur Norman born in December 25, 1935.
  • 1957 - Norman received a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).
  • 1979 - The Institute for Cognitive Science in UCSD was founded by Don Norman, Roger Schank, Allan Collins, and others.
  • 1986 - Norman introduced the term "user-centered design" in the book User Centered System Design: New Perspectives on Human-computer Interaction
